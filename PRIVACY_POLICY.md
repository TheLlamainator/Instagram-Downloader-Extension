# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 8, 2026

This privacy policy explains how the IGDown browser extension ("the Extension") handles user data. By using the Extension, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## **Data Collection**
The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All operations are performed locally in your browser.

## **Permissions**
The Extension requests the following permissions to function:

1. **`activeTab`**: Allows the Extension to interact with the currently active Instagram tab to extract media content.
2. **`downloads`**: Enables the Extension to save media files (images, videos, reels) directly to your device.
3. **`scripting`**: Injects scripts into Instagram pages to extract media URLs and metadata.
4. **Host Permissions**: Grants access to `https://www.instagram.com/*` and `https://instagram.com/*` to interact with Instagram pages.

These permissions are strictly limited to the Extension's functionality and are not used for any other purpose.

## **Data Usage**
The Extension processes Instagram page content locally in your browser to:
- Extract media URLs (images, videos, reels).
- Display media in the Extension's interface for download.
- Initiate downloads to your device.

No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

## **Third-Party Services**
The Extension does not use any third-party services, APIs, or external servers. All operations are performed locally.

## **Changes to This Policy**
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on the Extension's GitHub repository.

## **Contact**
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the GitHub repository's issue tracker.
